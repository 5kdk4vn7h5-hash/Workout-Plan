Priority Rules
01 — Protein First
On low-appetite days, hit 185g protein before anything else. Six training days means muscle protein synthesis is elevated constantly — feed it.
02 — Never Below 80g Fat
Dietary fat drives testosterone, joint health, and vitamin absorption. Going too low damages hormonal response and recovery quality.
03 — Recovery Is the Limiter
At six days a week, your results are capped by recovery, not effort. If lifts stall or you feel run-down, that's under-recovery — add a rest day or more food, not more volume.
04 — Track Waist, Not Scale
You're gaining muscle and losing fat at once — the scale misleads. Measure your waist weekly at the navel, first thing in the morning. That's the real marker.
