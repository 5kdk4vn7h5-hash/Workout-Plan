Priority Rules
01 — Protein First
On low-appetite days, hit 185g protein before anything else. Dropping 200 kcal from carbs is fine. Dropping 50g of protein costs you muscle.
02 — Never Below 80g Fat
Dietary fat drives testosterone, joint health, and vitamin absorption. Going too low damages hormonal response and recovery quality.
03 — Creatine, Increase Now
Move to 3–5g daily immediately. The current 1.5g dose is sub-therapeutic and providing near-zero benefit.
04 — Hydration
Minimum 1 oz per pound of bodyweight daily — about 243 oz (7.5 liters). Performance measurably drops when under-hydrated.
