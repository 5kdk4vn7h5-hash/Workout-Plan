Priority Rules
01 — Protein First
On low-appetite days, hit 185g protein before anything else. It protects the muscle you're building while you lose fat.
02 — Never Below 80g Fat
Dietary fat drives testosterone, joint health, and vitamin absorption. Going too low damages hormonal response and recovery.
03 — Adjust by the Waist
If your waist stalls for 2+ weeks, drop calories ~150/day. If strength and energy crater, add ~150/day. Let the measurement guide the math.
04 — Track Waist, Not Scale
You're gaining muscle and losing fat at once — the scale misleads. Measure your waist weekly at the navel, first thing in the morning.
